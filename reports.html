Availability Capacity Analysis

Shows how much of each carer's available working time is currently scheduled.

Use this report to:

  • Find carers with spare availability
  • Avoid over-scheduling staff
  • Assign extra visits or runs
  • Monitor carer workload balance

Area Capacity Analysis

Shows total care capacity across geographical areas by comparing staff availability with scheduled care hours.

Use this report to:

  • Check if an area has enough carers
  • Identify staff shortages
  • Decide whether you can accept new clients
  • Plan recruitment by location

Open Area Capacity Analysis

Contract Capacity

Compares carers' contracted weekly hours with the hours they are currently scheduled.

Use this report to:

  • Ensure staff receive their contracted hours
  • Identify under-utilised carers
  • Balance workloads across the team
  • Allocate additional shifts where needed

Open Contract Capacity
